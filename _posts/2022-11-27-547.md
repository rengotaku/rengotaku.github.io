[imgcat](https://iterm2.com/utilities/imgcat) のスクリプトを配置

```
$ vi /usr/local/bin/imgcat #<= スクリプトをコピペ
$ chmod 755 /usr/local/bin/imgcat
$ imgcat /path/to/image
```
